# veered
Veered is a mobile app for discovering hidden, unusual, and unforgettable places between Point A and Point B.

Instead of showing only the usual tourist stops, Veered helps travelers find the places most people drive right past — ghost towns, forgotten cemeteries, roadside oddities, movie locations, caves, historic markers, wildlife sightings, and other off-route gems. The goal is to turn every trip into an adventure by making it easier to explore the weird, the meaningful, and the unexpected.

Veered is built for curious travelers, road trippers, explorers, and anyone who has ever wondered, “What cool place am I missing right now?”

Vision

Veered exists to help people break away from boring, predictable travel and discover places with stories, mystery, history, and personality. It is about finding the stops that make a trip unforgettable.

Features
Discover hidden and unusual places nearby or along your route
Explore categories like ghost towns, cemeteries, caves, roadside oddities, movie locations, and more
Save favorite spots for future trips
Submit new discoveries
Turn everyday drives into memorable experiences
Why Veered?

Most travel apps focus on major attractions. Veered focuses on the places that feel personal, strange, historic, overlooked, and worth talking about later.

Our mission is simple: make every drive more interesting.
